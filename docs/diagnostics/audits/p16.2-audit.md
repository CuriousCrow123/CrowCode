- [ ] BUG-p16.2-1: `putchar('A')` on source line 4 is reported at line 5 instead of line 4. The instrumented source shows `__crow_step(4)` for `putchar('A')` (correct), but the dump shows Step 2 at Line 5 (wrong). This suggests the step numbering in the instrumented source maps `__crow_step(4)` to line 4, but the dump reports it as line 5.
- [ ] BUG-p16.2-2: `putchar('\n')` on source line 5 is entirely missing from the dump. The instrumented source has `__crow_step(5)` after `putchar('\n')`, but there is no step for it in the output. It appears the step for putchar('\n') was merged or skipped. The dump goes from Step 2 (Line 5) directly to Step 3 (Line 6), losing line 5's putchar entirely. This is likely because putchar('\n') produced only a newline which may have been merged with the previous IO, and then the step was collapsed. Either way, the step is missing from the trace.
