## Bugs Found

- [ ] BUG-p14.2-1: The `strcpy(dst, s)` call on line 10 has no visible step. The instrumented source shows `__crow_step(10)` after `strcpy(dst, s)`, but there is no step recorded for line 10 in the dump. Step 7 jumps directly to line 11 (len2 declaration). The strcpy step appears to be silently swallowed -- it produces no ops (since strcpy doesn't create or modify a tracked variable), so it may be that the step was emitted but collapsed/merged. However, for educational purposes a step should still appear on line 10 so the user sees the program executing strcpy.
